This website is a simple 2D simulation of satellites orbiting Earth and the main problem that arises due to space debris.

If an object collides with another object, it creates space debris. This space debris can then collide with more objects, creating more space debris.

This concept is called Kessler Syndrome, and it will eventually make an orbit completely unusable for satellites due to the risk of collisions.

Controls:
Click and drag to create a new satellite.
Press r to re-launch the last satellite.
